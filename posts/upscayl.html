What is UPScayl?

UPScayl leverages advanced AI algorithms to enlarge and enhance images. Unlike traditional upscaling tools that rely on interpolation, UPScayl uses neural networks to predict details, textures, and sharpness, giving your images a professional look. It is completely free, open-source, and works offline, making it perfect for privacy-conscious users and those with limited internet access.
